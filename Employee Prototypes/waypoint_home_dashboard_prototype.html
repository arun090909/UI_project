About Waypoint

Built to shorten the distance between good people and good jobs.

Waypoint started with a simple observation: most job boards optimize for volume, not fit. We built a platform that ranks opportunities by real skill match and real distance, and pairs it with local hiring events.

Employers post once and reach candidates who are actually qualified and nearby. Job seekers see fewer, better matches instead of an endless scroll.
