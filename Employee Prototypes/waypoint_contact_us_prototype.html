Common questions

Why am I not seeing job matches?▲
Matches come from the skills on your resume. Upload or update your resume from your Profile page, and make sure at least three skills are listed.
How do I cancel an event registration?▼
Go to your Profile page, find the event under Registered events, and select Cancel registration.
Can employers see my address?▼
No. Employers only see your city and distance, along with your resume when you apply to one of their jobs.
